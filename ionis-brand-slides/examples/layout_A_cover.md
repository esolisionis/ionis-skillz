# Example — Layout A (Cover / Title)

A worked example of the cover slide for an internal deck. Useful as a few-shot reference when generating Layout A specs.

This example pairs with the deck whose closing slide is in `layout_I_closing.md` — a co-presented internal deck about AI tooling at Ionis.

---

### Slide 1 — Cover

**Layout:** Layout A (Cover / Title)

**Title:** "Skillz."

**Body / copy regions:**

- **Title line:** "Skill" in black, "z." in solid Raspberry, 120pt Gotham/Arial Black. The "z" carries the visual stress — it's the spelling cue.
- **Subtitle / lede:** "Giving your AI conditional memory — and turning your know-how into something it can reuse."
- **Meta strip (above presenter block):** `AI DATA CLUB · 15 MIN · MAY 2026`
- **Presenter block (below the title rule):**
  - `**Eric Solis** · Director, Data Science & AI` (Raspberry left rule)
  - `**Chris Jerome** · Senior Systems Analyst` (Orange left rule)

**Visual spec:**
- Primary color anchor: Raspberry (the solid "z," the right-edge solid strip, presenter rules, and meta)
- Prow block composition: none on the cover; reserved for content slides
- Wing treatment: Cool Gray 1 watermark on the **left edge**, alt-cropped, ~17% slide width, opacity 1.0 (the asset is already light enough at 100%). Anchored to top-left, extending past the corner.
- Photography: none (internal/ops deck — no campaign photo). Background is solid white per brand book.
- Typography accents: solid Raspberry on the "z," on "Ionis" in the meta, and on presenter names

**No footer on this slide** — the cover owns its full canvas.

**Visual finish details:**
- Right edge has a 14px vertical solid Raspberry strip acting as a brand color anchor.
- 4-color Ionis lockup (`assets/logos/Ionis_Logo_web_HEX.svg`) sits in the top-left at ~140×56px.

**Speaker notes (optional):** "Yes, with a z. It's a callsign, not a typo — and the spelling cue is the only thing on the slide that's allowed to break the wordmark convention."

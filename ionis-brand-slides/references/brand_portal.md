## Data-visualization color rationale (from the portal)

The palette supports data storytelling four ways: **consistency** (same color across multiple visualizations makes the same thing recognizable in different views), **emphasis** (call out one element among many), **association** (bind variables to each other or to a concept), **mood** (set tone). Designer note: use colors as listed; do not convert PMS C to Process unless production demands it.
